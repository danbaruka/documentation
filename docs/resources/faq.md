## What is Safrochain?

Safrochain is an Africa-first Layer 1 blockchain built on the Cosmos SDK. It prioritizes African markets and diaspora corridors while welcoming anyone facing the same constraints: high remittance fees, thin banking access, and need for fast, transparent settlement.

## What is the SAF token?

SAF is the native token used for fees, staking, and governance. Total fixed supply is **1 billion SAF**.

## When is mainnet launching?

Mainnet is planned for **Q3 2026**.
