Safrochain empowers communities through **fast, affordable, and accessible
blockchain infrastructure** tailored for real-world use cases.

A **Cosmos SDK Layer-1** built for mobile-first users, everyday payments, and
real utility. Safrochain connects **local economies** to global markets while
making room for **local currencies** and **non-USD denominated stablecoins**
(so prices feel familiar, not “always in dollars”), with sovereign block space,
predictable fees, and IBC rails to the wider Cosmos ecosystem.

## What you’ll find here

- **Run a node**: install `safrochaind`, connect to testnet, and become a validator.
- **CLI reference**: common command patterns for keys, staking, queries, and transactions.
- **Modules**: Safrochain-specific modules and how they work.
- **Resources**: tokenomics, whitepaper, brand assets, and FAQ.

## Quick facts

- **Binary**: `safrochaind`
- **Token**: **SAF**
- **Supply**: **1,000,000,000 SAF** (fixed)
- **Denom**: `usaf` (`1 SAF = 1_000_000 usaf`)
- **Mainnet target**: **Q3 2026**
